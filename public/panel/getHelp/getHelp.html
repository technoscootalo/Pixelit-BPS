Panel Guide

This guide will help you understand how to use the panel effectively.


Accepting Forms

To accept account creation requests navigate to the Forms manager page. Review each submission carefully and click "" or "".
If the request reason is appropriate, the user is of age 13 and above and their Discord account is in the Discord server, accept that user. Low quality forms will be denied on the spot.


Muting Users

To mute a user, navigate to the User Managemenr page. Search for the user by their username.
Click the Mute button You can specify a mute duration after you click the Mute button in minutes or mute them permanently by just typing 0 as the mute duration.


Banning Users

To ban a user, navigate to the User Managemenr page. Search for the user by their username.
Provide a reason for the ban. Bans are typically permanent, but you may have an option to set a temporary ban.
You can specify a ban duration after you click the ban button in minutes or ban them permanently by just typing 0 as the ban duration.


Checking Audit Logs for Bad Words

To monitor for inappropriate language, navigate to the "Audit Logs" page.
The logs will show instances where potentially offensive words were used, along with the username, the sentence that they were going to type and the timestamp of when it was logged.
